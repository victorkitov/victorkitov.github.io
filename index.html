Victor Kitov

Lecturer, researcher, team leader.

Specialization

I have practical experience and give lectures in the areas of machine learning, deep learning, computer vision and neural style transfer.

Research

I lead a research team at Lomonosov Moscow State University, specializing on improving neural style transfer - a technology, where one image with some content needs to be automatically stylized using another image, representing the style.
